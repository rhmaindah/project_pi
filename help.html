Delivery Information

1. We now offer the ability to order online for pickup in our outlets.

2. Minimum 2 day lead time required

3. Momecake can selecting to pay with Cash or with Bank Deposit

4. If you want to pickup in outlet, our outlet team may contact you to confirm your order. Should an item not be in stock our outlet team will call to advise and suggest a replacement.

5. If you want to delivery, our delivery team may contact you to what time do you want to deliver the cake. Momecake have there are 3 delivery timeslots available 09:00 - 12:00, 12:00 - 15:00 and 15:00 - 18:00

6. Once the order has been paid, if you would like to cancel or change your order a minimum of 2hrs notice before your selected pickup time is required.
