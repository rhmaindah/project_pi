About Us

Momecake was founded in 2015 which is a homemade cake shop that sells a variety of cakes from sponge cakes, birthday cakes to Lebaran cakes. Our Mission is to make people smile. Such an honour to give a gift for someone and make them happy. The happiness we feel when creating emotional connections is why we exist till now. We love what we do and make a cakes is our way of bringing you a piece of that happiness and make memorable experiences with your friends and family.
